teaching

I teach and have taught a variety of quantitative methods, data science and text analysis related courses at undergraduate and graduate levels at the University of Vienna, University of Southern Denmark, University of Oslo, and the Copenhagen Business School, including the CBS Summer University. Along methodological courses, I also teach and have taught courses on the political psychology, behavioral economics for public policy, and evidence based public policy.

For several years, I taught Applied Multilevel Modeling at the ECPR Summer School for which I received the Cora Maas Award for the best evaluated course. I also taught introductory and advanced workshops especially focused on R and quantitative text analysis.
