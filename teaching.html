Past courses

Previously, I taught a variety of quantitative methods related courses mostly at graduate level at the University of Vienna, University of Southern Denmark, University of Oslo, and the Copenhagen Business School. Along methodological courses, I also taught courses on the psychological foundations of political decision-making. Furthermore, I taught introductory and advanced workshops especially focused on R and quantitative text analysis.

For several years, I taught Applied Multilevel Modeling at the ECPR Summer School for which I received the Cora Maas Award for the best evaluated course.
